class Inventory : public MonoBehaviour¶

Player inventory of weapons. Coordinates with the PlayerShoot and ShopScript scripts to update values. Also handles selling weapons from the inventory screen.

Public Functions

void FiscalShock.GUI.Inventory.hideWeaponInfo(int slot)

Callback for when the cursor is no longer hovered over a weapon in the inventory. Hides that weapon’s information.

Parameters

void FiscalShock.GUI.Inventory.updateWeaponSlots()

Update inventory slots to match the player’s actual available weapons. Also called by the ShopScript when the player purchases a weapon.

void FiscalShock.GUI.Inventory.sellWeapon(int slot)

Callback for clicking on an inventory cell (should be renamed, but won’t be, to avoid last-minute bugs from not updating all references). If the player is in front of the weapon shop, the weapon is sold. If the player is in the dungeon, the weapon will be equipped when the game is unpaused.

Parameters

  • slot: associated weapon slot

Add a weapon to the player’s inventory. Updates the PlayerShoot list of available weapons, as well as the inventory screen.
