void CreditRatingGUI.Awake()

Delayed update of the rating bar. There were race conditions when the bar was updated immediately. This does lead to the bar showing invalid data right after the player loads into the hub for the first time, if they pause immediately. It will be updated after 1 second passes while time is flowing.
