void FiscalShock.AI.DebtCollectorMovement.OnControllerColliderHit(ControllerColliderHit col)

Check for collisions. The controller collider was chosen, as it worked the best out of various colliders we experimented with. Unity’s physics engine is not the nicest to deal with.

Because this is based on controller collisions, collisions are only detected from the front! That’s okay, because the DC is always facing the player, except when stunned.

Parameters

  • col: information on the hit that occurred
