class PlayerHealth : public MonoBehaviour¶

Takes care of damage taken by the player and manages misc functions like invincibility and the player flashlight.

Public Functions

void PlayerHealth.resetVignette()

Update the reference to the vignette object and hide it.

void PlayerHealth.takeDamage(float damage)

Activates when the player is hit, if not dead the player loses money equal to the damage taken. If the player is out of money, they lose.

void PlayerHealth.endGameByDebtCollector()
IEnumerator PlayerHealth.enableIframes(float duration)

Enables invincibility when the player loads in.

Initialize references and the default state.

void PlayerHealth.OnCollisionEnter(Collision col)
IEnumerator PlayerHealth.showHitVignette(float duration)

Display the hit vignette HUD object (red effect) to inform the player that they have taken damage.

Parameters

  • duration: in seconds, how long to display the vignette

void PlayerHealth.Update()

Used to disable invincibility after players have a few seconds to position themselves, or if they try to attack an enemy.

Private Members

GameObject PlayerHealth.hitVignette

Reference to the hit vignette on the HUD.

float PlayerHealth.timeMultiplier = 0.01f

Modifier on how long to display the hit vignette, based on damage taken.

Light PlayerHealth.playerFlashlight

Reference to the player’s flashlight. The flashlight color is used to inform the player that they are invincible by coloring it green.
