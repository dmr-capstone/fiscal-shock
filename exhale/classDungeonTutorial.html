class DungeonTutorial : public MonoBehaviour¶

Displays a tutorial GUI when the player first enters the dungeon. The tutorial explains to the player what signs to look for to determine what objects are portals. Currently, portal models have a red animated arrow if they lead to a new dungeon level, or a green animated arrow if they lead back to the hub. Initial user feedback showed that players were unable to infer what each portal did without this instruction.
