void FiscalShock.Procedural.Dungeoneer.generateDelaunay()

Create a new, random Delaunay triangulation. The points on the Delaunay are found using a Poisson disc sampling algorithm. The Delaunay is used by the A* search of the Debt Collector, and also to create the Voronoi.

void FiscalShock.Procedural.Dungeoneer.generateVoronoi()

Generates the dual of the Delaunay triangulation, a Voronoi diagram. The Voronoi is used for the placement of environmental objects.

void FiscalShock.Procedural.Dungeoneer.generateRoomGraphs()

Determine the Mystery Dungeon-esque “rooms” and size them up as configured by the DungeonType. Works by picking a set of points on the Delaunay triangulation at random that are a given distance away from each other, known as the “master Delaunay points.” Then, the Delaunay triangulation of those points is found. From this secondary DT, a supergraph of a spanning tree is used to determine where corridors should exist. Additionally, rooms are created, which consist of a “seed point” (the master DT point) and a number of surrounding Voronoi cells.
