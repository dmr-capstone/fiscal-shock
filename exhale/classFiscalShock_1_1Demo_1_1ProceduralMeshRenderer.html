class ProceduralMeshRenderer : public MonoBehaviour¶

Visualizes the graphs used to generate the dungeon and handle A* pathfinding. Currently, you can toggle this on with F4 (see Cheats.cs), but you can only change what graphs to view while in the Editor, not builds.

Make sure you have Gizmos enabled in Scene view to view the graphs there.
